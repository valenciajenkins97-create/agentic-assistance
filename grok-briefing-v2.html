8. THE SPECIFIC PROBLEMS

Problem 1: Short Side Is Broken

STRONG_SELL has 50% accuracy with positive average returns. The system was calling shorts that went up. Root cause was the funding double-negation bug (now fixed), but we have zero post-fix short data. We don't know if shorts work now.

Problem 2: Component Weights Are Backwards

The two heaviest components (orderbook 0.36 + momentum 0.34 = 70% of score) show no predictive power. The best predictor (oiRegime at 60.3%) has only 3% weight. The system is dominated by noise.

Problem 3: Fees Eat the Edge

At 0.045% per fill (round trip ~0.09%), with a scalp TP of 0.5% and average wins of $0.034, fees ($0.009 avg per fill) represent 26% of each win. The edge is thin enough that fees are a material drag.

Problem 4: Position Sizing Escalated Into Losses

Started at $10 per trade, ended at $50 (entire account). No winning track record justified the increase. This is the single biggest risk management failure.

Problem 5: Wrong Quiet Hours

System avoids 0-6 UTC but trades at 07:00 (31% accuracy) and 15:00 (14.8% accuracy). The actual best hours are 08:00, 14:00, 18:00, 21-22:00.

Problem 6: No Backtest Framework

10,000 snapshots with components and 5,903 with outcomes ‚Äî enough data to backtest properly. But there's no walk-forward testing, no Sharpe calculation, no drawdown simulation. All parameter choices (threshold=38, Kelly fraction, TP/SL %) are arbitrary.

Problem 7: API Rate Limits Cause Data Gaps

Collector times out ~30% of runs. Coinglass OKX returns 50001. Coinbase rate limits. This means the engine frequently operates on stale or incomplete data. 1,595 of 10,000 snapshots are missing component data.

Problem 8: Overtrading Low-Confidence Coins

XRP was traded 16 times SHORT with 26.1% accuracy. AXS was traded 9 times after being blacklisted. The system had no per-coin performance tracking to stop trading coins that consistently lose.
