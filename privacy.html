We collect phone numbers provided directly by the account holder for the sole purpose of sending personal notifications and messages on their behalf. No data is collected from message recipients beyond their phone number.

Phone numbers are used exclusively to deliver text messages requested by the account holder. Messages include personal notifications, reminders, and communications to family and friends.

We do not share, sell, or distribute any personal data to third parties. Phone numbers are never used for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Phone numbers are retained only as long as needed to deliver messages. Recipients can request removal at any time.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Typically 1-5 messages per week per recipient.

Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support.
